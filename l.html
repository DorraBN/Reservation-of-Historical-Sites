Access instructions




In public transportation

The museum is accessible by subway lines 1, 2, and 5. Get off at the "Musée d'Histoire " station and take exit 4.

by car

Paid parking is available near the museum. The museum also has a parking lot for visitors, which costs 5 € for the day. You can also park on the street, but spaces are limited.

By bike

Self-service bike stations are available near the museum. You can also lock your bike on the street, but make sure to follow the parking rules.
